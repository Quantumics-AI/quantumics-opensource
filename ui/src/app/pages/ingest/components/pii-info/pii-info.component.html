Personal Identifiable Information [PII]:

PII is essential for personal privacy, data privacy, data protection, information privacy and information security.

Quantumics will auto identify PII columns in a file.

A column in a file can be marked or unmarked as PII by selecting the checkbox.

Marked PII column will not be shown in the ingested file.

Some of the PII's that are currently identified in a file by Quantumics.

  1. Credit Card - Visa
  2. Credit Card - Mastercard
  3. Credit Card - Amex
  4. Credit Card - Diner's Club
  5. Credit Card - Discover
  6. Credit Card - JCB
  7. Credit Card - Maestro
  8. Email - RFC5322 standard
  9. SSN
  10. Passport- UK
  11. NI Number
  12. Driving License UK
  13. IBAN Number
  14. Phone Number - ITUT Standard
  15. Phone Number - EPP Standard
